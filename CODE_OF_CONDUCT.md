# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone contributing to or participating in StackQuadrant.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and project

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. The project team reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
